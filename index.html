Our Story

Roan Café was inspired by the beauty of different shades, stories and people coming together. We wanted to create a calm and welcoming place where every visit feels meaningful.

Whether you are starting your morning, studying, working, or catching up with friends, Roan is made for slowing down and staying awhile.

Come for the coffee. Stay for the moment.
